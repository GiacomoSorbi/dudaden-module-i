Welcome to MY SKY BALLOON Website

Schedule balloon flights 7 days a week, at sunrise and sunset between mid April and mid October. Established since 1990, thousands of people have enjoyed a hot air balloon flight over the beautiful Kent countryside with Kent Ballooning.
As a local family run business we believe that quality of service is just as important as value for money. Our hot air balloon flights last around an hour and launch from exclusive locations throughout Kent, around the Canterbury, Maidstone, Folkestone and Ashford areas. After your balloon flight we celebrate with a traditional champagne toast or Spitfire for those who prefer beer, courtesy of our sponsors Shepherd Neame. Whether floating over the majestic spires of Canterbury Cathedral, looking down at the magnificent Leeds Castle or surveying the beautiful Kent coast line, Kent Ballooning is sure to provide an unforgettable experience. If you’ve ever wondered what it must be like, then now is the time to try. We look forward to flying with you soon.
